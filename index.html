The World of KIMÆRA

KIMÆRA (Pronounced Kim-Era) is a creature-centric, real-time RPG adventure game.

Created as a love-letter to 1990’s and 2000’s single-player creature-collector and adventure games, KIMÆRA takes genre conventions and skews them on a new axis!

Your Journey Starts Here!

In KIMÆRA, the whole Forista region is yours to explore! As you do, you’ll meet the people, wildlife, and ever-mysterious kimaera creatures that call it home. See where your journey takes you!

Meet Some Kimaera Creatures!

These creatures are known collectively as kimaera.

Kimaera are different from regular creatures, as they embody, command, or are made of, concentrated elemental matter. They cover the world, just as numerous as the animals, plants, and humans– maybe even more so. Each species of kimaera is unique.

Features

Fantastic Creatures!

Meet and befriend over 100 different species of kimaera! 

Unfolding Storytelling!

Explore a narrative story through the eyes of Sage, who, along with their friends, sets out with courage to explore Forista!

Cataloguing Everything!

Fill out your encyclopedia with creatures, and expand what you know about them!

Encountering New Friends!

Encounter and befriend kimaera in the wild, and discover unique palettes for each kimaera depending on where they’re found!


Strategizing with Your Squad!

Spar with other teams throughout the region in action-based, real-time combat! Opponent squads, wild kimaera, apex kimaera, and story combat– do any of them to gain experience and get stronger!

Relaxing in Nature!

Sparring not your thing? Take it easy and relax in one of the garden locations, where you can hatch eggs, raise kimaera, and earn experience that way!

FAQ

How do I pronounce the game name?

Kim-Era.

How many KIMAERA creatures will there be?

Over 100 species will be in the game! KIMAERA will look different depending on where you encounter them in Forista, too.

Can the KIMAERA creatures talk?

The creatures in KIMAERA (who are also named kimaera) can talk the same languages as us! They can think, create, dream, and rationalize just like you or I. In some cases, they can surpass even human levels of skill!

Can I customize my player character?

In KIMAERA, you'll be controlling and playing as Sage, our main character. Although Sage can't be customized, they have an important story to tell, and they're very much their own person! I hope you enjoy meeting them and their friends.

Can I name my kimaera creatures?

Yes. You may change this any time in your squad menu!

Is this game single-player or multi-player

KIMAERA is a single-player, offline game.

What gender are my kimaera creatures?
What do they like and dislike?

Although all named characters in the story have defined identities and genders, kimaera creatures that you befriend in the wild in your playthrough have no game-defined gender or identity, so you as a player are free to let your headcanons reign supreme.

Each kimaera creature you befriend has traits depending on both their species and their individual quirks. Don't be afraid to find out what they like and dislike through experimentation!

Are there lootboxes or microtransctions in KIMAERA?

No.

When is the game releasing? Can I play a demo?

A demo of the game is available here. Watch this website and wishlist us on our Steam to find out when we're releasing KIMAERA!

What game engine do you use?
Are you a company or an indie team?

Our game is built in GameMaker Studio 2. We are an indie team and we put a lot of love into our work!

How can I support the development of the game?

For supporting development, head to our Patreon page!

Demo, How to Play, & Controls

How to Play & Controls
Speak to people you meet to progress the game!
Try to help out the people you meet.

You can explore and walk around Forista with the ARROW KEYS.
And you can speak to people or inspect things with the SPACE BAR.

Hit the ESC KEY to bring up the menu to quit the game.

*Gamepad Support will be integrated into Combat-Based Sandbox Demo builds.
Machine Requirements
KIMÆRA’s Sandbox Build requires a Windows 10 or 11 device with a keyboard to play.

KIMÆRA will run on most computers, but our minimum requirements are:
Windows 10 (or above), 1.2 ghz of processing power, 4 GB RAM, 512 mb video memory, DirectX Version 10, and 2 GB of storage space available.
