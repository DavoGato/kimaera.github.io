Your Journey Starts Here!

KIMÆRA (Pronounced Kim-Era) is a creature-centric, real-time RPG adventure game.

Created as a love-letter to 1990’s and 2000’s single-player creature-collector and adventure games, KIMÆRA takes genre conventions and skews them on a new axis!

The World of KIMÆRA

In KIMÆRA, the whole Forista region is yours to explore! As you do, you’ll meet the people, wildlife, and ever-mysterious kimaera creatures that call it home. See where your journey takes you!

Meet Some Kimaera Creatures!

These creatures are known collectively as kimaera.

Kimaera are different from regular creatures, as they embody, command, or are made of, concentrated elemental matter. They cover the world, just as numerous as the animals, plants, and humans– maybe even more so. Each species of kimaera is unique.

Features

Fantastic Creatures!

Meet and befriend over 100 different species of kimaera! 

Unfolding Storytelling!

Explore a narrative story through the eyes of Sage, who, along with their friends, sets out with courage to explore Forista!

Cataloguing Everything!

Fill out your encyclopedia with creatures, and expand what you know about them!

Encountering New Friends!

Encounter and befriend kimaera in the wild, and discover unique palettes for each kimaera depending on where they’re found!


Strategizing with Your Squad!

Spar with other teams throughout the region in action-based, real-time combat! Opponent squads, wild kimaera, apex kimaera, and story combat– do any of them to gain experience and get stronger!

Relaxing in Nature!

Sparring not your thing? Take it easy and relax in one of the garden locations, where you can hatch eggs, raise kimaera, and earn experience that way!

FAQ

Will you be releasing the engine?

The engine will remain non-public for the foreseeable future. Our game is built in GameMaker Studio 2, and the game itself was coded by a total of just three people so far. As we may modify the engine and code for future projects, we feel it’s best to keep it closed-source.

Are you a company or an indie team?

We are a small indie team! We simply use Shanty Studios LLC to help get our works protected and copyrighted! We remain small because our budget is small, and we put a lot of love into our work! All money this project has ever had put into it came from our own pockets.

Demo, How to Play, & Controls

How to Play & Controls
Speak to people you meet to progress the game!
Try to help out the people you meet.

You can explore and walk around Forista with the ARROW KEYS.
And you can speak to people or inspect things with the SPACE BAR.

Hit the ESC KEY to bring up the menu to quit the game.
Machine Requirements
KIMÆRA’s Sandbox Build requires a keyboard and a desktop or laptop computer to play.
The supported release is for Windows.
