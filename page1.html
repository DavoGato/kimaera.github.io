Additional Copyright Information

KIMÆRA (Pronounced: Kim-Era) is a video game designed by Dave Saunders (AKA DavoGato).

KIMÆRA is dedicated to every daydreamer and storyteller, with the hope they will create the stories they dream to create, no matter how long ago the dream began.

KIMÆRA is being created, directed, illustrated, written, and by Dave Saunders (AKA DavoGato) in assistance with a small team of supplementary programmers, writers, musicians, and artists. These individuals are listed in the credit section.

This game is a work of fiction, wherein you encounter amazing creatures and explore the ecological wonders of the Forista region, a place within the KIMÆRA video game.

Sandbox builds are provided on this website to showcase gameplay elements to the public prior to a completed story. As this project is updated on the systems backend, the free, share-ware sandbox build will be updated as well. Please keep in mind this project is in ongoing development, and elements of the builds, story, and gameplay may change over time.
